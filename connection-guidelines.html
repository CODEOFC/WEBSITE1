Guidelines for Temporary and Permanent Connections

Temporary Connections:

  • Step 1: Contact GRENLEC to schedule an appointment.
  • Step 2: Provide necessary documents and payment.
  • Step 3: Await approval and confirmation.

Permanent Connections:

  • Step 1: Submit an application for a permanent connection.
  • Step 2: Coordinate with GRENLEC for site inspection.
  • Step 3: Pay the required fees and provide necessary documents.
  • Step 4: Schedule installation of poles and lines.

Electricity Connection by GRENLEC

If you are looking for detailed information about electricity connection services provided by GRENLEC, please visit the GRENLEC website. GRENLEC offers a range of services and guidelines for connecting to the power grid.
