Electricity Safety Tips

At Home:

  • Always unplug appliances when not in use.
  • Avoid overloading electrical outlets.
  • Keep electrical cords away from water sources.

At School:

  • Teach children about electrical safety.
  • Ensure proper maintenance of electrical equipment.
  • Report any damaged or exposed wiring immediately.

At Work:

  • Follow all safety procedures and guidelines.
  • Use personal protective equipment when necessary.
  • Know the location of emergency exits and fire extinguishers.

Additional Safety Tips:

  • Never touch electrical wires with wet hands.
  • Regularly inspect electrical cords for damage.
  • Turn off appliances before unplugging them.
