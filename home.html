Overview of Pole Installation Process

Our pole installation process involves the following steps:

  1. Site Assessment: We assess the location to determine the need for new poles.
  2. Pole Placement: New poles are strategically placed based on assessment results.
  3. Wiring: Electrical wiring is installed on the new poles to provide power.
  4. Quality Check: We ensure the installation meets safety and quality standards.
  5. Connection: Once installed, you can connect to the power grid.

For more detailed information, you can visit our Connection Guidelines page.
